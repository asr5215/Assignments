Plot:

Dr. Strange follows the tale of a narcisistic neurosurgeon who in a tragic car accident experiences the loss of his hands. In his search to fix his hands, he meets the Ancient One. She is a celtic woman located in Kamar-Taj who helps him to regain the use of his hands through magic. Dr. Strange must then use his knowledge of magic to defeat a foe of the Ancient One.

Featured Actor:
